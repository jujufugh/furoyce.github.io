## Put It Into Practice (Hands-On Lab)

### Monitor and Optimize Oracle SaaS Apps and OIC Business Flows with Log Analytics [HOL3232]

**Details & RSVP:**  
[https://reg.rf.oracle.com/flow/oracle/ocw25/catalog/page/catalog/session/1750723607536001SAg9](https://reg.rf.oracle.com/flow/oracle/ocw25/catalog/page/catalog/session/1750723607536001SAg9)

This interactive hands-on lab (HOL) will show you how to monitor business processes and integrations built with Oracle Integration Cloud (OIC) and connected SaaS applications—helping you identify issues faster, improve business flow reliability, and deliver better user experiences.

With built-in machine learning, advanced search, and intuitive visualizations, you’ll discover how easy it is to move from raw logs to actionable insights.

You’ll simulate real-world scenarios and apply process monitoring techniques to:

- **Monitor End-to-End Business Flows** – See how log-based monitoring gives you the context you need to track key transactions.
- **Accelerate Troubleshooting with AI** – Use log pattern recognition and anomaly detection to isolate issues quickly.
- **Gain Cross-App Observability** – Connect the dots across OIC and SaaS ecosystems like ERP, HCM, and CX.
- **Empower Business + IT Collaboration** – Dashboards turn technical data into business insights, bridging the gap between IT operations and business teams.
